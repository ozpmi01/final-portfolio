About Me

Hello! I am Phrenstien Alberto, a passionate student on multimedia arts and willing to explore on my capabilities. My designs focuses on creating simple yet tells a story, it blends traditional art techniques with modern technology. I constantly seek to push creative boundaries through projects in animation, graphic design, photography, and immersive media. As a multimedia art student, I do not just create visuals but I build experiences. I am always chasing that moment where something clicks, not just visually but emotionally. Art is my interface, the world is medium.
